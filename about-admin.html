🎓 About Us

Welcome to EventLine IGDTUW!

This platform has been created to bring all event-related information to one place. Whether it's a guest lecture, cultural fest, orientation program, tech talk, or society showcase — we bring it all to your fingertips. You’ll find the details here — including date, time, venue, and more.

🌟 Our Commitment

At IGDTUW , the auditorium is more than just a venue — it's where inspiration, creativity, and knowledge come alive. Our goal is to ensure no one misses out on important happenings and to make it easier for everyone to stay connected with campus life.

💡 What We Offer

  • 📢 Live Event Updates
  • 🗓️ Event Schedules & Descriptions
  • 📬 Student Society Announcements
  • 🖼️ Post-Event Highlights & Media
  • 🔔 Notifications for Upcoming Programs
